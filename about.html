About Nomadic Excursions

Nomadic Excursions is made up of a team of about 30 passionate individuals, and some wonderful partners located in the places that we travel.

Our team is based out of Seattle, Washington, but many of us will join trips to make sure things go according to plan. We look forward to seeing you out there!

While we can't introduce you to everyone, here are three people you might meet on your trip!
